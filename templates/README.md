
## Formatting

 * `<info></info>` green
 * `<comment></comment>` yellow
 * `<question></question>` black text on a cyan background
 * `<alert></alert>` yellow
 * `<error></error>` white text on a red background
 * `<fire></fire>` red text on a yellow background
 * `<notice></notice>` blue
 * `<heading></heading>` black on white
